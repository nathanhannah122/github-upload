Star Wars is an American space media franchise created by George Lucas, which began with the 1977 film, A New Hope and quickly became a pop-culture phenomenon, with a large fanbase all across the world.

There are 12 films in total, 9 from the skywalker saga and 2 anthology films.

As well as films there are a range of books, comics and video games based on the franchise.
